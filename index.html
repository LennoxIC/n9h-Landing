Product

One platform. A swarm of specialists.

n9h isn't a single model with a bigger context window — it's a coordination layer that routes work to the right kind of intelligence, every time.

Orchestration Core

Routes work across a swarm of specialized agents, balancing speed against rigor automatically — no manual hand-off required.

Persistent Memory

Vector and graph memory mean every agent remembers what came before — no repeated context, no cold starts on the next session.

Composable Tools

Plug in your APIs, internal docs, and dashboards — agents call them like any other teammate would, with full audit trails.

Capabilities

What the agents are actually doing

Six capabilities work together under the hood. None of them are exposed as separate products — they're just how n9h thinks.

Research Intelligence

Pulls from the web, your docs, and prior conversations to build a sourced brief in minutes, not days.

Strategic Planning

Breaks ambiguous goals into ordered, dependency-aware task graphs before a single agent starts working.

Multi-Agent Collaboration

Agents hand off work, challenge each other's outputs, and converge on a verified answer before it reaches you.

Advanced Reasoning

Chain-of-thought and self-critique loops catch logical errors before they ever reach your screen.

Automation Workflows

Trigger-based pipelines run agents on a schedule or in response to events — no human required to press start.

Knowledge Synthesis

Merges scattered, conflicting sources into one coherent, cited answer — with disagreements flagged, not hidden.
